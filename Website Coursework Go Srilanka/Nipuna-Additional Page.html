Sigiriya-The Lion Fortress



Description

Sigiriya is a one of the most popular tourists attractive place located in Central province, Sri lanka. It is a rock, which is a UNESCO listed world heritage site. The historical , location , view in the top are couse to sigiriya become a popular tourists destinations in Sri Lanka.

History

Sigiriya was built by King Kashayapa in 15th century. The high of this rock is 590 ft. On top of the sigiriya are the ruins of a palace. In the inside of the rock there are colourful paintings as wall arts known as Sigiriya Frescoes. And sigiriya is a example for ancient sri lankan urban planning.
